Welcome to Seethio Trip Planner

Whether you're drawn to the ancient rock-hewn churches of Lalibela, the stunning Simien Mountains, or the bustling markets of Addis Ababa, our trip planner is your gateway to crafting an unforgettable adventure.

Activities to explore !

Hiking and Trekking

Explore the stunning landscapes of the Simien Mountains, Bale Mountains, or the Gheralta Mountains in Tigray for exceptional trekking experiences.

White-Water Rafting

The Omo River and Blue Nile offer thrilling white-water rafting experiences, with varying levels of difficulty.

Birdwatching

Ethiopia is a birdwatcher's paradise with diverse bird species. Visit the Rift Valley Lakes or the Bale Mountains for birdwatching excursions..
