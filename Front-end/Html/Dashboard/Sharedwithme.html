    Day 1: Addis Ababa

    Arrive in Addis Ababa, the bustling capital of Ethiopia, and dive right into the vibrant atmosphere of the city. Explore the National Museum to see the famous Lucy, a 3.2-million-year-old hominid, and visit the Holy Trinity Cathedral, a masterpiece of Ethiopian architecture.

    Day 2: Lalibela

    Fly to Lalibela, home to the remarkable rock-hewn churches. Spend two days exploring these ancient wonders, witnessing the devotion of pilgrims, and hiking in the surrounding hills for panoramic views of the town.

    Day 3-4: Gondar

    Travel to Gondar, often called the "Camelot of Africa." Explore the medieval castles, visit Debre Berhan Selassie Church with its iconic angel-painted ceiling, and wander through the vibrant markets.

    Day 5: Bahir Dar and Lake Tana

    Drive to Bahir Dar and embark on a boat trip on Lake Tana, the largest lake in Ethiopia. Explore the island monasteries, known for their ancient religious manuscripts and tranquil surroundings.

    Day 6-7: Simien Mountains

    Journey to the Simien Mountains, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Hike through this rugged landscape, spot unique wildlife, and enjoy stunning views of the Ethiopian highlands.

    Day 8-9: Axum

    Head to Axum, an ancient city known for its obelisks and historical significance. Visit the St. Mary of Zion Church and explore archaeological sites to uncover the mysteries of the Aksumite Empire.

    Day 10: Addis Ababa

    Return to Addis Ababa for a farewell dinner, and perhaps catch a traditional dance performance to celebrate your journey.
